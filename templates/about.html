About Good First Issue Finder

What does this tool do?

Good First Issue Finder helps new contributors find appropriate issues to work on in open-source projects. It analyzes your GitHub repositories to identify your top programming languages, then searches for "good first issue" labeled issues in popular projects that match your skills.

How it works
  1. Enter your GitHub username
  2. We analyze your public repositories to determine your top 3 programming languages
  3. We search for open issues labeled as "good first issue" in repositories that:
    • Use one of your top languages
    • Have at least 50 stars (to ensure they're established projects)
  4. We present a list of issues tailored to your skills
Privacy Information

This tool only accesses your public GitHub information. We don't store any data about you or your repositories. All processing is done at the time of your request and no information is saved.
